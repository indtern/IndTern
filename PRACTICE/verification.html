Who can use this page?

  • Candidates: Confirm that your certificate is registered with IndTern.
  • Employers / HR: Validate certificates before hiring or shortlisting candidates.
  • Colleges / Institutions: Verify completion for students who submit IndTern certificates as part of their records.

What happens after you submit?

  1. We check if the Certificate ID exists in our records.
  2. We match the candidate name and domain (if provided).
  3. You or the requester can be contacted if clarification is needed.
