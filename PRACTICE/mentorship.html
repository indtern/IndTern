1‑on‑1 Online Mentorship

Personal mentorship to guide your career, skills & projects

IndTern’s 1‑on‑1 mentorship sessions are focused, personalised calls where you can discuss your career confusion, domain selection, learning roadmap, resume, LinkedIn, projects, or interview preparation directly with a mentor.

  • Get clarity on the right domain and next steps
  • Get honest feedback on your profile & projects
  • Discuss a simple, practical learning roadmap
  • Prepare for internships and job interviews
Apply for 1‑on‑1 Mentorship

No batch, no crowd — just you and the mentor for 45–60 minutes.

Session Snapshot

  • Mode: Online (Google Meet / Zoom)
  • Duration: 45–60 minutes
  • Language: English / Hindi
  • Type: Single or multiple sessions

What we can work on together

  • Choosing the right domain (Web, App, Data, AI, etc.)
  • Understanding what skills are actually needed in industry
  • Creating or improving your resume & LinkedIn profile
  • Reviewing your existing projects and suggesting improvements
  • Planning a clear learning roadmap for 1–3 months
  • Preparing for internship / placement interviews

Example use‑cases

  • “I’m confused between Web, Data and AI — what should I choose?”
  • “My resume looks empty. What projects should I build?”
  • “I have done some courses, but I don’t know what to do next.”
  • “I am not getting interview calls. What am I doing wrong?”
  • “I want someone to check my portfolio and give real feedback.”

Who is this for?

  • Students confused about which domain or path to choose
  • Freshers who want to build a strong profile for internships/jobs
  • People who have done courses but need direction & clarity
  • Students stuck in projects and needing guidance to complete them
  • Anyone who prefers personal guidance instead of generic videos

What you’ll walk away with

  • A clearer idea of which direction suits you
  • A short‑term roadmap (what to do in the next 30–60 days)
  • Profile improvements you can implement immediately
  • Specific resources or project suggestions (where relevant)
  • More confidence about your next steps

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the same as the internship program?

No. The 1‑on‑1 mentorship is a separate service. It is a focused personal session where you discuss your doubts and goals directly with a mentor.

Do I need to be very experienced to attend?

No. We work with complete beginners as well as students and freshers who already have some knowledge or projects.

Can I book more than one session?

Yes. You can start with a single session and later choose additional sessions if you feel it is helpful.

Will you provide a certificate for this?

No certificate is provided for the 1‑on‑1 session. The purpose is guidance and clarity, not certification.

How will I know my slot is confirmed?

After you apply, we will contact you via WhatsApp/email with available timings. Once you confirm, we’ll share the final meeting link.

What if I need to reschedule?

You can inform us in advance, and we’ll try to arrange a new slot based on availability.

Ready to get personalised guidance?

Share your details and goals, and we’ll schedule a 1‑on‑1 mentorship session for you.
