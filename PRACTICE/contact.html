Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some common questions. You can always contact us if you need more clarity.

How do I apply for an internship?

You can apply using our main application form via the “Apply Now” button on any page. Choose your preferred domain and submit the required details.

Do I get a certificate after completion?

Yes, on successful completion of your internship, you receive a certificate that can be verified using our Certificate Verification page.

Are all internships online?

Most of our opportunities are remote or flexible, so students from different locations can participate. Specific details will be mentioned in each internship description.

Can colleges partner with IndTern?

Yes, we welcome collaborations with colleges and training institutes. Use the form above and select “College / Institution” as your type.

Want to start an internship with IndTern?

You can apply directly using our application form or contact us if you have any questions before applying.
