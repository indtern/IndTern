About IndTern

We’re on a mission to make real‑world experience accessible to every student.

IndTern was started with a simple belief: talent should not be limited by a lack of opportunity. We help students and freshers gain practical exposure through meaningful internships and structured learning.

What we do

We connect learners with internship opportunities, skill‑based programs, and verifiable certificates that actually hold value in the job market.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academics and industry by offering accessible, structured, and practical internship experiences across multiple domains.

Our Vision

A future where every student, regardless of location or background, can showcase real projects, gain confidence, and step into the professional world with clarity and purpose.

Our Story

IndTern was created after seeing how many students struggled to get their “first chance” – even when they had skills and motivation.

Understanding the problem

Many students had theoretical knowledge but found it difficult to get practical exposure or convincing proof of their skills when applying for jobs.

Building a structured path

IndTern was started to provide organized internships and guided tasks, so learners can build portfolios and gain confidence step by step.

Focusing on real‑world readiness

Our programs emphasize practical work, responsibility, and communication skills – not just theory or certificates.

Growing with our learners

As more students and freshers trust IndTern, we continue to expand our domains, improve our processes, and collaborate with industry professionals.

Why IndTern?

We are focused on delivering real value, not just another certificate.

For Students & Freshers

Get guided tasks, flexible schedules, and a clear structure so you can learn while managing college or other commitments.

  • Multiple domains to choose from
  • Skill‑based hands‑on work
  • Verifiable certificates

For Recruiters & Companies

Access motivated learners who are already exposed to basic workflows, tools, and responsibilities in their chosen domain.

  • Better prepared candidates
  • Evidence of completed work
  • Transparent verification process

For Colleges & Institutions

We support colleges that want to add practical exposure to their students’ journey through internships and projects.

  • Structured internship plans
  • Clear documentation
  • Online certificate verification

Our Core Values

These principles guide every internship we design and every learner we support.

Authenticity

We believe experience should be earned through real tasks and honest effort, not shortcuts.

Accessibility

Opportunities should not be limited to a few. We aim to keep our programs reachable for students from different backgrounds.

Growth Mindset

We encourage learners to experiment, make mistakes, and improve with every project and interaction.

Transparency

Clear communication, transparent processes, and verifiable records are central to how IndTern operates.

Registered & Recognized

IndTern is registered under MSME, reinforcing our commitment to structured, credible internship opportunities for learners and early professionals.
